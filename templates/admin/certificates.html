How to use

  • Click Generate next to a student to preview their certificate in a new tab — then use Ctrl+P or browser print to print it.
  • Tick multiple students and click Download Selected as ZIP to get all certificates in one zip file.
  • Print on A4 paper in landscape orientation for best results.
  • After printing, have the Principal / Director sign the signature line and apply the official school stamp in the circle provided.
  • To set a completion date or grade for a student, edit their enrollment under Enrollments.
